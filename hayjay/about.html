SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION

The first is search engine optimisation, or SEO , which helps you promote your business in the unpaid search results. The second is search engine marketing, or SEM , which lets you buy ad space in the search results. SEO is all about getting your site in front of the right people who are searching for your products and services
Now, there are lots of ways to do this, we’ll explain them in detail later on, but the key is knowing what words people actually type in - the keywords. They are the most relevant words to your business.
Understanding these will help you improve how you show up when these words are searched.
